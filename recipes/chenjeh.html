Chenjeh Kebab

Pages: Home | Chenjeh Kebab Recipe | Sultani Kebab

There are two kinds of people in this world: people who’ve had chenjeh, and people who are one bite away from questioning every kebab decision they’ve ever made. Kabab Chenjeh is basically what happens when beef cubes go on a luxury vacation involving saffron, onions, butter, and open fire. It’s smoky, juicy, slightly dramatic, and traditionally cooked by at least one uncle who insists he alone understands charcoal. The smell alone is enough to make neighbors “randomly” visit around dinner time.

Serves:

  • 4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg beef sirloin or ribeye, cut into large cubes
  • 2 large onions, grated
  • 3 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 4 tbsp brewed saffron
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp olive oil
  • 50 g melted water
